How to cook Sevai?
	Seavi is a indian sweet that we prepare generally during festival.

Ingrediant
Milk - 1/2 litre
macaroni(sevai) - 1/2 cup
sugar - As needed
cardemon - 1 pinch
raisins - 5 piece
cashew - 4 piece

break cashew into pieces. Now boil the milk, when milk started boiling add the macaroni , cardemon, raisings, broken cashew.
After 5 minutes add sugar. Now stir the mixture for 10-15 minutes.

Here the sevai is ready to serve.
